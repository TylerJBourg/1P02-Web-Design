What is Shroud of Darkness

Shroud of darkness is a point and click detective, mystery and adventure game based in a futuristic cyberpunk world. Where the world governments have all united under one corrupt leader Devereux that is secretly bent on the destruction of the common people. As the anti-hero Aileron you must go around the city solving murders and mysteries all well trying to discover Devereux’s master plan and avoiding Devereux’s evil elite top secret hit squad code named Icarus.

Future Free Expansion

Missing Shadow Expansion

This expansion takes Aileron into the secret criminal underground under the city of Leander. Where Aileron must search for and solve the disappearance of his friend and eye in the sky Torrin while unable to rely on any tech and assistance support leaving Aileron to have to survive and fin missing friend with only his wit and skill.
