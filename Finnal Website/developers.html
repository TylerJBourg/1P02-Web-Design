About Everest Studios

“Everest Studios was founded on the belief that when you buy a game you should receive a completed game with all the content included in one purchase no DLC, no microtransactions, no loot boxes; one purchase should equal one amazing experience” – Jason Coburn

The studio was founded in 2011 by a group of former developers that were fed up with the current gaming market and how games were published and developed so they decided to start their own studio that would-be consumer friendly.

The Minds Behind Everest Studios

Jason Coburn the Founder, CEO and Creative Director of Everest Studios (pictured left) has been in the gaming industry for 25 years and has been playing games for 30 years. He is best known for working on titles like Star Wars Battlefront one (2004) and two (2005), Tom Clancy’s Rainbow six Vegas Two (2008) and Fallout New Vegas (2010).

Phil Young is the lead Level and Character Designer at Everest Studios and has been a part of the studio since its creation in 2011. Phil has been a Designer for games for about 10 years now and has work on notable games such as World of Warcraft (2004), Elder Scrolls Oblivion (2006), and Call of Duty: Black Ops (2010)

Mason Morata is the lead Animator and Environment artist at Everest Studios and joined the studio in 2013. Mason has been an Animator for about 23 years now and has been an environment artist for about 14 years now. Mason is best known for working on animations for DC animated movies and has also worked in japan on animations such as Attack on Titian, Dragon Ball Z and Naruto. Games he has animated for include Uncharted (2007) and Assassins creed Brotherhood (2010)
